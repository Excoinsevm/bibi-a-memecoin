The Story behind BIBI

On May 8th, over 2,000 people discussed launching a Chinese-focused MEME token with zero taxes on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) through a Twitter space conversation.

Initially, the token, called BIBI, was meant to be launched by a popular Chinese influencer (KOL). However, due to technical issues, there was a four-hour delay. Eventually, someone from Taiwan, now known as Mr. Zhou, took over and successfully launched the token.

The community's desire was to have the token without any taxes, with the liquidity pool burned, the contract renounced, and no team or marketing wallet tokens.

BIBI, represented by a cute phoenix, symbolizes the immortal phoenix rising from the east, representing something that will last forever. This vision was shared by the initial 2,000 participants who wanted to create something in the cryptocurrency world that would endure, with agreement from the community.

Impressive achievements have been accomplished so far:

  • Trading Volume: 20 million
  • Token Holders: 23,000+
  • Volunteer Builders: 200+
  • Telegram: 7.4k (CN) & 2.3k (global)
  • recorded on May 30th, 2023.

The community remains highly confident, and with the support of a strong community, they aim to reach many more significant milestones in the future. The goal is to become well-known worldwide as the leading meme token on the BSC chain.

BIBI Foundation

BIBI Foundation is a DAO initiated by BIBI holders. Its mission is to promote the development of the BIBI community and sustain the progress of the BIBI ecosystem. The funds are donated from volunteers who will become members of the Foundation after donating, and proposals would be voted by all the members fairly before being implemented.

  • Proposal System: Members have the opportunity to propose ideas to benefit the BIBI project, which are discussed and voted upon by all contributors.
  • Community Support: The foundation actively supports the BIBI community through various initiatives.
  • Events: The foundation organizes events like the NYC conference, bringing together enthusiasts and stakeholders.
  • Volunteering Efforts: Founders and members actively volunteer to help the BIBI community.
  • Membership: Individuals can join the foundation by donating 100 USDT.
